“Freedman”

发表于 2022-06-12 更新于 2023-02-20 本文字数： 560 阅读时长 ≈ 2 分钟

I could name you “the Freedman”.[1]

A trumped-up charge was given by you, born as male.

The incident happened between the sufferers and the criminals instead of male and female. Indeed, I can understand that the continuous epidemic had been corroding the heart of everyone then inevitable incident was caused.

I have known that we normal male is the Eldian[2] with born guilty from the public comments. However, no Eldian can choose to be born as Eidian or not, it is a slippery slope of logic to allow people who have not made moral choices to bear the moral consequences. By contrast, the trumped-up charge imposed through the issue on gender is Arabian Nights(天方夜谭).

Some Freedmen commented in another novel way—after the incident had happened, only can the girls rethink, but boys do nothing. With the assumption that the “girls” represents the sufferers, if it is necessary for the victim to reflect, it certainly supports the “victim guilt theory” that you oppose; why should you reflect when you are not guilty?

Some males are unable to contain themselves then compare the incident of Handan[3], but the Freedmen replied interestingly—“Six girls can never beat to death a boy while a boy can easily rape and kill a girl.” So, you want to show the mercy of your ethnicty, the Freedman, don’t Is male said to be the tool of raping and killing? Is there only raping and killing in your world?

The extreme comment is that “all men ought to die including my father.” It is so rediculous!

Our common enemy is the Mafia-like gangs(黑帮) instead of ourselves. What we should rethink is the reason of the generation of them and why they are so rampant over the legislation instead of continuous infighting. Admittedly, there are quite a few instances in the development of this society over thousands of years where certain aspects of society have proven to be biased in favor of men, and this can be a reason to pursue equal rights for men and women, but not to the extent that it needs to be imposed on all men, and it is even more elusive to be the handle of the Freedman.

The world is not an utopia with Babel tower(巴别塔). Everyone should unite to counteract the darkness. The friends living in Shanghai nowadays must know who is really responsible to such events and secondary issues. They are never male, female, any Freedman or the Freedman. Don’t cover eyes to ignore the real truth. There would be no difference between you Freedman and the Mafia-like gangs who attack the people unless we stop the war of comment.

This incident let me know the quality of the people around me amply. Facts speak louder than words and justice will prevail. The existence of gossip depends on whether you take care of it. I am thankful that no extreme comments belong to these dearest friends, they are still kindhearted, self-confident and hopeful, know the right from wrong. I sincerely hope my friends and I live happily, be away from rubbish.
